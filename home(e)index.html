An E-Learning Platform is a digital ecosystem designed to facilitate the online learning process for students and instructors. It provides access to educational content, tools for interaction and collaboration, and systems to manage user progress and certification. This platform aims to make learning flexible, accessible, and engaging by utilizing online resources, interactive tools, and multimedia content.
